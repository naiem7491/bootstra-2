Modern design, inspired by our interior design experts

Enhance your space with Acupanel® - our acoustic slatted panel designed for walls and ceilings.
Inspired by contemporary, Scandinavian design, these modern slat panels are loved by interior designers and homeowners alike. As a feature wall in your living room, or a statement ceiling feature, these panels can instantly elevate your space.

The UK's original out-of-the-box slat wall panelling solution

Innovation is at our core, as is listening to our clients. They wanted modern wood panelling that was quick to install, and we listened.
Using over 50 years of wood knowledge and expertise, in 2017 we developed the first DIY, wood slat wall panelling solution in the UK. Our simple, yet easy to install wall and ceiling panel was born, and is available today in a range of finishes, to continually upgrade spaces with quality wood textures with ease.

Outstanding, class-A acoustic properties
